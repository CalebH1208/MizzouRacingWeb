Vehicle Dynamics

What We Do...

Our suspension and chassis subteams make up the vehicle dynamics team. We design and manufacture components in house with our manual and CNC machines. Some highlights of our suspension package are KW V5 dampers, adjustable anti-roll bars, and carbon fiber rods. The team adjusts the toe and camber for events to maximize performance through extensive testing.

Vehicle Dynamics Sub Team:

Our vehicle dynamics sub team is managed by the dynamic duo of our breaks lead, Dylan Goldstein, and our aerodynamics lead, Ryan Blanken. The two of them have been studying suspension geometry and chassis design while updating the components on the cars. Their goals for the year include a rear strucutre update, upright redesign, tire models, and data acquistion to make on track adjustments.
