Aerodynamics

What We Do...

The subsystem works to design and manufacture aerodynamic components for the racecar. With the help of our sponsors, we can manufacture our aerodynamic package completely in house. The package creates a downforce on the tires and in turn alters the maximum frictional force the car’s tires can generate. Components include front and rear wings, an undertray, nosecone, and sidepods.

Sub Team Lead: Ryan Blanken

Ryan is a junior mechanical engineering major. Joining the team in Fall 2023 he has dedicated his time to designing, validating, and manufacturing the car's aerodynamic package. Ryan has designed the front wing, rear wing and so much more. In addition he also works with other members to design other parts of our aerodynamics package. Ryan is also one of our two vehicle dynamics leads.
