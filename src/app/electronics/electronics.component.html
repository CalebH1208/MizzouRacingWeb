Electronics

What We Do...

The Electronics Team is responsible for the design, manufacturing, testing, and integration of all electronic modules across the internal combustion and electric cars. An in-house custom wiring harness connects modules such as a MoTeC M150 ECU, custom Power Distribution Module, custom Wireless Live Telemetry, and custom Dashboard, among other components. The electrical components on each car provide necessary control systems for cooling, ignition, fuel, shifting, power limiting, and data acquisition.

High Voltage

What We Do...

The High Voltage team is responsible for the EV powertrain and supporting electronics. The EV powertrain consists of an Accumulator-the system’s high voltage battery pack, the Traction Inverter, and the Drive Motor. The Accumulator stores 7.249kWh of energy at 480V, while being able to source 315A of DC current, all from 18650 Li-Ion cells. While competition limits us to 80kW, our system can provide up to 110kW - 147HP and 170 lb-ft of torque at the motor.

EV Systems

What We Do...

The Ev Systems sub team designs, manufactures, tests, and integrates the high voltage electrical systems for our electric vehicles. This includes the design and manufacturing of the Tractive Battery Container, the integration of the high voltage powertrain, and the design of all high voltage safety systems.
